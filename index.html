What is Rust?

When you join Rust for the first time or after a wipe, you are sleeping on the beach, with a stone and a torch.
The first thing you can do is go farm resources to setup a base, in your base you can place a toolcupboard, boxes, traps, workbenches and a lot more. When your base is up, you can go outside to farm more resources for a stronger base or you can go PvP with people to get more loot. If you have explosives, you need to research those, you can go raid people by destroying their base. The most common end goal of a wipe is having a strong base, having researched a lot of items (if the next wipe isn't a blueprint wipe) and have a lot of loot.
